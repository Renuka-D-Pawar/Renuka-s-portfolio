BusBuddy: Smart Complaint & Query Hub 🚍⚡

This Complaint & Query Web App is a user-friendly platform designed to address and resolve bus-related problems efficiently. Whether it's delays, safety concerns, or missing buses, this app allows commuters to raise complaints and get quick resolutions. 🏙️📢

🚀 What Makes It Stand Out?
✅ Easy Complaint Registration – Submit bus-related issues in just a few clicks. 📝🔍
✅ Real-Time Tracking – Monitor the status of your complaint and get updates. 🚀📡
✅ Query Resolution System – Ask questions about bus schedules, routes, or facilities and get instant answers. ❓✅
✅ Transparency & Efficiency – Connects users with transport authorities for quick actions. 🚍⚡
✅ User-Friendly Interface – Simple, accessible, and mobile-friendly design for all commuters. 📱🚌

💡 Why is It Special?
Unlike traditional complaint systems, this app ensures direct communication with bus authorities, providing fast issue resolution and improving public transport experiences! 🚏📈
